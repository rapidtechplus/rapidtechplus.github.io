# Rapid Tech Plus — Product Requirements

**Version:** 1.0
**Status:** Active
**Last updated:** 2026-07-13

---

## 1. Vision

Rapid Tech Plus is a modern software company building high-quality SaaS products,
AI-powered solutions, developer tools, and enterprise software.

This website is the company's **permanent** official presence. Although the first
release primarily supports company and bank verification, it is architected from
day one to grow into a full marketing and product site without redesigns or
architectural rewrites.

## 2. Primary Goals

- Establish a professional company identity
- Build trust with clients and partners
- Support business / bank verification
- Showcase company services and software products
- Demonstrate engineering excellence
- Maintain excellent SEO and performance

## 3. Non-Goals (v1)

No authentication, database, CMS, admin panel, backend APIs, blog engine,
contact-form backend, analytics dashboard, or customer portal. The site is
fully static.

## 4. Core Principles

Static · extremely fast · SEO-optimized · mobile-first · accessible · modern ·
professional · easy to maintain · component-driven · future-scalable.

## 5. Design Philosophy

Quality bar comparable to Vercel, Stripe, Linear, Resend, Clerk, and Supabase.

**Embrace:** minimal, premium, spacious layouts; beautiful typography; smooth,
subtle motion; excellent responsiveness.

**Avoid:** template-looking or generic agency design; excessive animation; bright
flashy colors; large paragraphs; cluttered UI.

## 6. Success Criteria

Lighthouse scores ≥ 95 for Performance, Accessibility, SEO, and Best Practices.
The codebase should remain maintainable for years without major rewrites.

## 7. Verification-Relevant Content

Every deployment must clearly present:

- Company name — "Rapid Tech Plus" — consistently across all pages
- What the company does
- Contact details (email; phone once a real number exists)
- Legal pages: Privacy Policy, Terms of Service
- Consistent branding and canonical URLs
