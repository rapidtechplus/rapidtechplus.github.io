# Vision

## The company

**Rapid Tech Plus** is a modern software studio building web platforms, SaaS
products, custom applications, and — increasingly — AI-powered solutions for
growing businesses. We combine product thinking with disciplined engineering to
ship software that is fast, maintainable, and built to last.

## What this website is

The **permanent** official presence for the company. The first release exists to
support company and bank verification, but it is architected from day one to grow
into a full marketing and product site — without redesigns or rewrites. See
[`../PRD.md`](../PRD.md) for the formal product requirements.

## The experience we want to create

A visitor should immediately sense **engineering excellence, trust, innovation,
and professionalism**. The site should feel like premium SaaS software (Vercel,
Stripe, Linear, Resend, Clerk, Supabase) — never a generic agency template.

## Three-year direction

- **Today:** company profile, services, products preview, contact, legal pages.
- **Near term:** dedicated Industries and Technologies pages; a stronger, honest
  AI-capabilities story; product detail pages generated from `content/`.
- **Later:** Careers, Documentation, Blog/Insights, Case Studies, Open Source,
  Press Kit — each added as a new route reusing existing sections and content,
  with no architectural change.

## AI positioning

AI is presented as a **capability, not a marketing claim**. As the company's AI
work matures (AI product development, agents, LLM integrations, RAG, workflow
automation, MCP servers, intelligent search, assistants), the site surfaces it
with concrete, credible framing — never exaggeration.

## Guiding constraints

Static-only, extremely fast, accessible, SEO-strong, and maintainable for years.
Quality and longevity always win over speed of implementation.
